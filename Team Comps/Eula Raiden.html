Eula Raiden is a team comp that involves Eula and Raiden themselves, along with supporting units. Eula's cryo element reacts with Raiden's electro element to cause superconduct, lowering enemy's physical resistance.

Rotation:
Eula E (Tap) -> Raiden E -> Diona E (Hold) -> Xinyan E -> Eula Q -> Eula E (Hold) then NA until burst explodes -> Repeat
