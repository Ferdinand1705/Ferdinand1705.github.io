Permafreeze

Kamisato Ayaka

Main DPS

Kaedehara Kazuha

Anemo Debuffer/Support

Sangonomiya Kokomi

Healer/Support

Shenhe

Buffer/Support

Permafreeze is a team comp where enemies are aimed to stay frozen during the period of Ayaka's Q duration. The word permafreeze refers to "Permanent Freeze".

Rotation:
Ayaka E -> Kazuha E (Hold) then Q -> Kokomi E -> Shenhe E (Tap) then Q -> Ayaka Sprint to apply cryo to enemy then Q, then NA, CA and E until burst ends -> Repeat
