Anemo Debuffer/Support

Sukokomon is a team comp where lots of elemental reactions happen simultaneously. Sucrose serves as the swirl machine here spreading elements to surrounding enemies.

Rotation:
Fischl E -> Kokomi E -> Xiangling E -> Sucrose E -> Xiangling Q -> Kokomi Q until burst ends -> Fischl Q -> Xiangling E -> Sucrose E & Q -> Repeat
