Taser

Sangonomiya Kokomi

Main DPS

Beidou

Sub DPS

Xingqiu

Healer/Support

Fischl

Buffer/Support

Taser is a team comp that makes use of electro-charged reactions by constantly attacking anemies with hydro and electro simultaneously.

Rotation:
Kokomi E -> Fischl E -> Xingqiu E & Q -> Beidou E & Q -> Kokomi Q until burst ends -> Fischl Q -> Repeat
